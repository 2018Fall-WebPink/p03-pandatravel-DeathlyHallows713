Haunted Alcatraz Prison

Alcatraz is the most well-known prison in America, and there's a good reason why. It's a massive abandoned prison isolated on an island of its own with no means of escape. It held some of the most infamous criminals to have ever lived, such as Al Capone and Machine-Gun Kelly. Most prisoners who went through Alcatraz's gate either left their sanity behind in the penitentiary or never left at all. Some say the spirits of the prisoners still haunt the place to this very day.

More Stories About Alcatraz

Alcatraz is perfect for those who love a spooky haunted ghost tour, but history-lovers also enjoy learning about the rich past of the island and its inhabitants. The creepy atmosphere is the ultimate place to go spirit hunting, but it's also a great spot to go for a history lesson.

What else is there to do here?

Near the island is the city of San Francisco, where you can go sailing, take tours of the city, fish, etc. San Francisco also has plenty of hotels, restaurants, and shops to explore.
